SNAP 2023

A candidate aspiring to join a select Post Graduate (PG) programme of Symbiosis International (Deemed University) [SIU] will have to register and appear for Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Test 2022 For more information, please visit (www.snaptest.org)  An Indian national / NRI candidate will have to apply through SNAP to seek admission to a select PG programme of SIU.

 An NRI candidate (having graduated / graduating from a recognised University in India), after having applied through SNAP, has the option of consideration of her/his candidature as a national student or under the NRI category. In case an NRI candidate chooses to apply under the NRI category, s/he will have to further apply through Symbiosis Centre for International Education (SCIE). For more information, please visit www.scie.ac.in

However, a Foreign National / OCI / PIO / NRI candidate (having graduated / graduating from a recognised foreign University) is exempted from appearing for SNAP and has to apply through SCIE (www.scie.ac.in)
